Sining Laban sa Katiwalian

“Sining Laban sa Katiwalian” is a creative movement that integrates various art forms including visual, applied, literary, and performative arts to reveal the social, cultural, political, historical, and economic realities of corruption in the Philippines.

What is "Sining Laban sa Katiwalian"?

"Sining Laban sa Katiwalian" is a 3-day online art exhibit held from December 4-6, 2025 as ZC11's project in Art Appreciation. The exhibit aims to expose the realities of corruption in the Philippines through visual, applied, literary, and performance art. By the end of this museum experience, viewers are encouraged to reflect, stay aware, and uphold social responsibility.
